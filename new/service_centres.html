FE's New Corporate Headquarter Opening Ceremony 8pm 8th August 2011!
 
FE's new Corporate Headquarter Opening Ceremony held at 8 minutes past 8 pm on 8th August 2011 was attended by a huge crowd of over 3,000 of FE's committed Leaders, VIPs and invited guests, with many from overseas countries such as Philippines, Thailand, Indonesia, India, Taiwan, Hawaii, Singapore, China, Hong Kong, Mexico etc.

The spacious 40,000 square feet new Headquarter, which is double the size of FE's previous Headquarter, includes huge Warehouse Facilities, Admin Offices, BEP Training Rooms (the largest can accommodate 250 people), Members Business Lounge with Cafetaria, Pool Table and Massage Area, private meeting rooms for Leaders, a Gym, an Indoor Badminton Court, a Grand Foyer and lots of parking spaces! FE's new Headquarter will enable us to service our Leaders and Members better! No MLM company in Malaysia has such comprehensive facilities to match what we have today!

Over the last 6 years FE has grown exponentially, from a humble 2,000 square feet office in Cheras, to this new 40,000 square feet Headquarter with 60 Country Licensees around the world and manufacturing/warehousing facilities and a 10,000 square feet marketing office in Shenzen, China. This is a testament to the quality of FE's Leaders who have worked hard and relentlessly over the years to build FE into the global force it is today with over 2 million members worldwide and to become the largest seller of scalar energy products in the world today! To all of you, we THANK YOU and we look forward to an even more bountiful future together!
